Cheaha Creek
This is where the creek hits Lake Chinnabee. The tree on river right is onbvious until it fall into the lake one of these days. Across the water from the tree, the second rock on river left is just poking out of the water. This the low side of medium, or maybe an OK low level. If the rock is covered it is a nice level.
