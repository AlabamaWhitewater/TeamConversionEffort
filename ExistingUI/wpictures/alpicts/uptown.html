About 1995 Tony Baker ran the old mill slide at about 900 cfs.


His narration: "That was the fastest thing I've ever been on..."


(photo courtesy Tony Baker)
"... I had to change my line right at the bottom to square up perpendicular to the edge of the hole , It was getting shallow and I couldn't get much purchase with my paddle ..."

(photo courtesy Tony Baker)
"...Even at that it blew me back on the stern deck when I went through it . Haven't tried it a second time, the hole is just too ugly and obviously a keeper. Being older and wiser , I would not run it again myself , regardless of levels. I have since heard that there is some rebar in the rock . It would be a bad scene to hit some going that speed."
