Notes: Friends of the Locust          
Not run too often.  Lots of flatwater, but has a neat, short gorge section.  Put in around Cold Branch Hollow, if such a thing exists. The gorge has a 5' slide drop followed by a few smaller drops. In the last 2 miles or so, you encounter junkyard rapid, where you will find a glassy front surfing wave which only gets better as the water rises. Towards the end, you pass through the Kings Bend racecourse. Take-out at the normal put-in at 79/231 bridge (gage station on map).  Put-in where the road comes close to the river about 10 miles upstream of the take-out.  Requires a bit more water to be fun than the lower sections.  A really fun introductory tandem section.

The put-in above involves lots of flatwater, and is on the south/river left side. Alternate access may exist on the north/river right side. Consult thy map and good luck.
