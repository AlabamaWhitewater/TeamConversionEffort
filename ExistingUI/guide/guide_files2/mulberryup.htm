Notes:            
A fun run with play for any ability level given fairly high water. All the rapids are down the gut, but the waves can get big. Even at 2 feet there is some nice front surfing. It can take a lot of water, I used to consider 3' on the old 31 bridge to be minimum.

The USGS Garden City gage is 3.3' higher than the 31 bridge gage.
