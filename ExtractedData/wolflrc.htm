Run: Wolf (LRC) Creek Section: Class: V+ Put-In: Canyon Rim Rd Gradient: 280 Take-Out: Little River Length: 0.55 Precip Gage: Shuttle: Rt. 176 & a map Delorme Gazeteer: P. 27 A7 Water Q: Primary Gage: none Links: TOPO MAP Required Level: visual PICTURES Indicator Gage: LRC (USGS) TRIP REPORT Required Level: Notes: This Wolf has no really reliable gauge. You need to look at the creek to check if it is running. Realize the canyon will probably be at a scary level and you will be running Deep Throat at some screaming high level. Wolf Creek is hard to describe adequately. It is a typical very steep limestone sandstone creek. Their are several drops that I'd advise portaging. A few are probably not runnable, or at least not really healthy to run. If you are good enough to make the creek just remember that it flows into the Little River about a quarter mile below Humpty Dumpty.
