Run: Locust Fork of the Warrior River Section: Upper Class: II Put-In: CR1 (Five Pts Rd) Gradient: 9'/mile Take-Out: 231/79 bridge Length: 10 Precip. Gage: Albertville / West Fork Little Shuttle: CR1/Blackwoods Bend/US231 Delorme Gazeteer: P. 25 F8/F9/E8 Water Q: Primary Gage: Locust at Cleveland 231/79 bridge Links: TOPO MAP Required Level: 3.0' PICTURES Indicator Gage: TRIP REPORT Required Level: Notes: Friends of the Locust Not run too often. Lots of flatwater, but has a neat, short gorge section. Put in around Cold Branch Hollow, if such a thing exists. The gorge has a 5' slide drop followed by a few smaller drops. In the last 2 miles or so, you encounter junkyard rapid, where you will find a glassy front surfing wave which only gets better as the water rises. Towards the end, you pass through the Kings Bend racecourse. Take-out at the normal put-in at 79/231 bridge (gage station on map). Put-in where the road comes close to the river about 10 miles upstream of the take-out. Requires a bit more water to be fun than the lower sections. A really fun introductory tandem section.[PARAGRAPH_BREAK] [PARAGRAPH_BREAK] The put-in above involves lots of flatwater, and is on the south/river left side. Alternate access may exist on the north/river right side. Consult thy map and good luck.
